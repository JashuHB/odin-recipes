Spaghetti

Description

A saucy noodle dish with meatballs

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • tomato puree
  • meatballs
  • sugar
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. begin boiling water and cooking noodles
  2. cook meatballs
  3. simmer meatballs in tomato puree
  4. add dash of spices and sugar to taste
  5. combine noodles with meatballs and sauce, serve
