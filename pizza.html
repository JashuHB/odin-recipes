Pizza

Description

A delicious cheesy saucy dish easily shared and enjoyed worldwide

Ingredients

  • dough
  • tomato puree
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesean cheese
  • romano cheese
  • olive oil

steps

  1. knead dough
  2. add tomato puree, enough to lightly cover dough
  3. blend all mozzarella with a tablespoons each of the other cheeses
  4. add 2 tbsp olive oil spread over cheese
  5. bake at 450 F for 15 minutes
