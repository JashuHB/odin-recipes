Lasagna

Description

A saucy layered noodle dish with meat and cheeses

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato puree
  • mozzarella
  • ricotta
  • meat
  • sugar
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder

Steps

  1. begin boiling water and cooking noodles
  2. brown meat
  3. simmer meat in tomato puree
  4. add dash of spices and sugar to taste
  5. layer noodles with sauce, ricotta, then mozzarella, then repeat for several layers
  6. cook at 350 F for an hour
